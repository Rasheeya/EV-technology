Implementation FAQs

Common questions about transitioning to a smart city framework.

How long does a city-wide deployment take?

Deployments are phased to minimize disruption. A foundational IoT grid and central dashboard can be established in 6-8 months, while full autonomous integration and renewable grid retrofitting typically spans 2-5 years depending on the city's size.

Is citizen data secure on the STACKLY platform?

Absolutely. We utilize military-grade quantum encryption and decentralized blockchain ledgers to ensure all citizen data is anonymized and strictly protected against cyber threats. Privacy is embedded into the core architecture.

Can existing legacy infrastructure be integrated?

Yes, our platform is designed to be backwards compatible. Through edge-computing API gateways, we can connect legacy traffic lights, water systems, and power grids to the central AI, upgrading them without full physical replacement.
